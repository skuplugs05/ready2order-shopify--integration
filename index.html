Understanding Ready2order and Shopify

Ready2order is a cloud-based POS system designed for small and medium-sized businesses, particularly in retail and hospitality. It simplifies billing, inventory tracking, and customer management.

Shopify, on the other hand, is one of the leading eCommerce platforms that allows businesses to create and manage online stores with ease. When these two powerful systems operate independently, managing inventory and orders can become time-consuming and prone to errors.

This is where SKUPlugs steps in—bridging the gap between Ready2order and Shopify with seamless automation.

What is Ready2order Shopify Integration with SKUPlugs?

SKUPlugs is a reliable middleware solution that connects Ready2order POS with Shopify stores. It enables real-time synchronization of products, inventory, and orders between the two platforms.

Instead of manually updating stock levels or transferring order data, SKUPlugs automates these processes, ensuring that your business operations run smoothly and accurately.

Key Features of SKUPlugs Integration

1. Real-Time Inventory Sync

SKUPlugs ensures that whenever a product is sold in-store or online, inventory is updated instantly on both platforms.

2. Automated Product Upload

Upload products from Ready2order to Shopify automatically, including SKU, price, images, and descriptions.

3. Order Synchronization

Orders placed on Shopify are automatically pushed to Ready2order, eliminating manual data entry.

4. Multi-Store Management
